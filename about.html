About The Author

Hi. Thanks for taking the time to read this. As you may have guessed I'm a bit of an amateur (complete with github page template). Anyway...

"Who the heck are you?"

Well I'm a 30 year old (and will remain so for the rest of my days!) professional software developer, but unlike many in my field, I've only been employed for a little over 10 months (as of writing this). Now I say 'unlike many' because in my (rather limited) experience, I've noticed that most of the guys in my field are very experienced. But in contrast I've only just started out. At my age (OK. I'm not that old!) its a bit strange.

"Why 30? What happened?"

I actually started out in retail. It literally took my wife to ask me one day: 

"Do you want to do this for the rest of your life?"
This shocked me - genuinely. For years I had coasted through things. I'd never actually considered that this would be the job. That would be it. It made me reconsider a lot of things. Anyway, long story short by the end of the week I'd had enrolled in university on a course that combined Computer Science with project management - and 3 years later... Now I know my situation isn't unique. I can imagine there are any number of people who have taken the plunge to do something like this. But for me, its been rather liberating to leave behind what I was doing and take the chance on this - I don't regret it one bit. I'm now in my dream job.

"What Can we Expect?"

What you're likely to see on here are experiences, advice and some of the knowledge that I'm managed to gain. What you're not going to see on here is perfection. I'm a simple man and I'm still very much learning as I go. Please catch me on twitter(@derek_ling) if you twit. But otherwise, glad to have you around for the ride. You look lovely today.
